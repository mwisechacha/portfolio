Breast Cancer Detection System

View Project

Project Nina is a system designed as an AI diagnosis assistant for radiologists, helping to analyze mammograms and provide crucial insights. It predicts whether a tumor is malignant or benign, the BI-RADS score based on various mammogram features, and recommendations to guide diagnosis.

The Convolution Neural Network analyzes the mammogram and predicts the tumor type, the Random Forest Classifier takes addition tumor features, breast density, mass margin, and the CNN prediction, to predict the BI-RADS score. Radiologists review and approve the diagnosis, ensuring AI-assisted yet human-verified accuracy.
